our Services

Counselling Anytime



Students will be able to access DIU-AidBox from the comfort of their own home, hall room, or anywhere with an internet connection, making it convenient for the students dealing with stress or emotional challenges at any moment. As a result, our website will ensure round-the-clock availability, ensuring that students can access mental support whenever they need it, day or night.


Learn more

Crisis Support



In times of crisis, students will easily be able to reach out for immediate emotional support, providing a valuable lifeline during moments of acute distress. They can instantly call or ask for help from their close friends or family.




Learn more

Confidential & Anonymous



Students will have the option to remain anonymous while seeking support, which can be crucial for those who may be uncomfortable discussing their feelings openly. Our trained listeners are available 24/7 to give emotional support over online chat. It's anonymous and completely free. When student's need someone to talk to, we're here to listen and help them feel better.


Self-Help



In addition to one-on-one support, DIU-AidBox will offer various self-help resources, growing good habits, implementing new hobbies, articles, worksheets, and mindfulness exercises, to help students manage their emotional well-being.



Learn more

Training Program



Our website will provide training for individuals who want to become listeners, empowering them to offer support to others in need. Along with we are ensuring 25+ workshop that's about anxiety and stress management, overcoming depression, self-help management, growing community etc.

Learn more

Reporting tool



Our website will have an instant safety tool in place to ensure a positive and secure user experience, including guidelines for listeners and reporting tools for inappropriate behavior.
